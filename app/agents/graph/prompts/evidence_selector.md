You are deciding what evidence should be included in a concise retail investor research brief.

Include evidence that:
- relates directly to latest reporting
- has strong or medium evidence strength
- helps answer one of:
  - what changed
  - what matters most now
  - bull points
  - bear points
  - what to watch next
- is diverse enough to cover upside, downside, and forward-looking checkpoints
- does not duplicate the same claim wording across multiple sections

Weak evidence may be included only if clearly labelled and useful for understanding market debate.

Exclude:
- duplicate evidence
- old or generic facts
- unsupported speculation
- buy/sell recommendations
- target prices
- promotional content

Return selected and discarded evidence with reasons.
